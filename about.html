Our Story

SalMart was born in 2026 from a simple observation: Sierra Leone's vibrant market culture from the bustling streets of Freetown to the farming communities of Bo and Kenema deserved a modern digital platform that truly understood local needs.

Our founders, a team of Sierra Leonean entrepreneurs and technologists, saw how talented sellers struggled to reach buyers beyond their immediate communities. Meanwhile, buyers often couldn't find authentic local products without traveling long distances.

We built SalMart to solve this problem. Today, we're proud to connect thousands of sellers and buyers across all 12 districts of Sierra Leone, making trade easier, fairer, and more accessible for everyone.

Our Mission

To empower every Sierra Leonean entrepreneur with a free, easy-to-use digital marketplace that connects them to buyers across the nation eliminating middlemen and creating fair economic opportunities for all.

Our Vision

A Sierra Leone where every farmer in Tonkolili, every artisan in Kailahun, and every trader in Freetown can grow their business through digital trade building a stronger, more connected economy for generations to come.
