How SalMart Works

From creating your account to completing your first sale — it's simple, fast, and free. Here's everything you need to know.

For Sellers

Start Selling in 4 Simple Steps

Whether you're a farmer, artisan, or trader, getting started on SalMart takes just minutes.

01

Create Your Free Account

Sign up in under 2 minutes with just your name, phone number, and district. No credit card required, no hidden fees — ever.

  • Choose your role: Seller, Farmer, or Artisan
  • Verify your phone number via SMS
  • Get your verified seller badge instantly
02

List Your Products

Take a photo, add a price in NLE, write a description, and you're live. Our smart system helps you create professional listings in seconds.

  • Upload up to 5 photos per product
  • Set your price in New Leone (NLE)
  • Choose your category and location
  • Products go live instantly
03

Connect with Buyers

When a buyer is interested, they'll reach you via our in-app chat or WhatsApp. Negotiate price, delivery method, and timing directly.

  • Real-time chat with buyers
  • WhatsApp integration for easy communication
  • Secure messaging protects your privacy
04

Complete the Sale & Get Paid

Deliver your product, receive payment, and leave a review. Build your reputation and watch your business grow.

  • Flexible payment: cash, mobile money, or bank transfer
  • Arrange delivery or pickup as agreed
  • Both parties leave reviews to build trust
  • Top sellers get featured placement

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SalMart really free to use?
Yes! Creating an account and listing products is 100% free. We don't charge commissions or monthly fees. We believe in empowering Sierra Leonean entrepreneurs without financial barriers.
How do I get verified as a seller?
Verification is automatic once you confirm your phone number via SMS and provide valid identification. Most sellers are verified within minutes of signing up.
What payment methods are supported?
We support cash on delivery, Orange Money, Afrimoney, bank transfers, and other mobile money services popular in Sierra Leone. Sellers and buyers agree on the payment method directly.
Can I sell from any district in Sierra Leone?
Absolutely! SalMart covers all 12 districts — Western Area, Bo, Bombali, Bonthe, Kailahun, Kambia, Kenema, Kono, Moyamba, Port Loko, Pujehun, and Tonkolili. We connect sellers and buyers nationwide.
How do I handle delivery?
Delivery is arranged directly between buyer and seller. You can offer pickup, local delivery, or use third-party delivery services. Many sellers use motorcycle taxis (okada) for local deliveries.
