Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

How do I create an account?
Click "Sell Now" or "Create Account" on our homepage. Fill in your name, email, phone number, and select your district. You'll receive an SMS verification code. The whole process takes less than 2 minutes!
Is SalMart really free?
Yes! Creating an account, listing products, and browsing is 100% free. We don't charge commissions or monthly fees. We believe in empowering Sierra Leonean entrepreneurs.
How do I verify my seller account?
Verification is automatic after you confirm your phone number via SMS. You'll receive a verified badge on your profile within minutes.
What payment methods are available?
We support cash on delivery, Orange Money, Afrimoney, bank transfers, and other mobile money services. Buyers and sellers agree on payment method directly.
How do I report a problem with a seller or buyer?
Use the "Report" button on any user's profile or transaction. Our trust & safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours. You can also email us at safety@salmart.sl.
Can I sell from any district in Sierra Leone?
Absolutely! SalMart covers all 12 districts. Whether you're in Freetown, Bo, Kenema, Makeni, or any other district, you can sell to buyers nationwide.
How do I delete my account?
Go to your account settings and click "Delete Account." This action is permanent. Alternatively, email us at support@salmart.sl and we'll help you.
Do you have a mobile app?
Our platform is fully mobile-optimized and works on any smartphone browser. A dedicated mobile app for iOS and Android is coming soon!
